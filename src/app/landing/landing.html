Portal Excellence

Engineered for Modern Organizations

StaffHub brings enterprise-grade security and geolocation tracking to eliminate buddy punching and simplify daily operations.

Employee Self Service (ESS)

Empower employees to swipe in/out, monitor active hours, submit leaves, check regularization status, and download payslips independently.

HR Administration

Maintain dynamic employee records, publish company holiday calendars, view system activity logs, and oversee portal configurations effortlessly.

Approval Queue

Manage a real-time request workflow allowing administrators to review, approve, or reject employee leave and swipe regularization applications.

Payroll Automation

Calculate employee net payouts based on regularized shifts, customize allowances and deductions, and issue detailed monthly salary slips.
